About ZedAuto

Who We Are

ZedAuto is Zambia’s leading digital automotive platform that connects car owners, garages, and dealers through innovation, transparency, and technology.

“We’re not just changing how people service their cars — we’re redefining how Zambia drives.”

Founded with the vision to revolutionize Zambia’s automotive sector, ZedAuto brings convenience to your fingertips — whether you’re booking a service, finding a trusted mechanic, or exploring cars for sale.

Our platform is designed with the user in mind — combining sleek technology, reliable data, and excellent service to keep you moving.
